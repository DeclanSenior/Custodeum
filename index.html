Controls:

p - pause

click unit - select unit

click after clicking unit - move unit

double click enemy while standing in range - clean enemy

click outside movement and cleaning range or right click - deselect janitor

click janitor after moving - deselect janitor

+/- or scrolling - zoom in/out

WASD/Arrow keys - move camera

Left shift - toggle enemy movement range highlight

Right shift - hold enemy movement range highlight


Text Tutorial:

The game is split into two phases: player phase and enemy phase. In player phase, each janitor is given a chance to move and potentially start cleaning a mess. To do so:

  1. Select a janitor by hovering over it and clicking.
  2. After selecting a janitor, click another square within the blue highlighted area to move janitor to that location.
  3. Double click an enemy in range of janitor to engage cleaning (range can be seen in stats panel).
  4. If you do not plan on cleaning an enemy, click the janitor again after moving to end its turn early.
  5. Right click or click outside movment range to cancel selection.

During enemy phase, each enemy can move and potentially target a janitor. After all enemies have moved, return to player phase.
